West End Curling Centre – Sign up as a “Future Participant”

West End Curling Centre is working with supporters and partners to build a new curling facility in Central Etobicoke to satisfy the demand of curlers. If you’d like to secure your spot when this centre opens(est. 3-5 years), sign up now and confirm with payment just prior to opening.

Benefits:

  • Secures one of a limited number of spots in the new centre
  • Access to weekly league play and other free ice opportunities (once opened)
  • Access to WECC and other Toronto Curling Association club bonspiels (in the interim)
  • Keep informed about developments with West End Curling Centre.

Fill in the registration form below to save your spot at West End Curling Centre!

Future Participant Agreement

I agree to join West End Curling Centre as a "future participant" to play and learn the game. I understand once opened the centre will charge annual fees for curling that are competitive with other local curling programs plus additional fees for multiple leagues and capital. Initial financial payment will not be required until shortly before opening of the new centre (estimated at 3-5 years). I have the right to remove my name from this list at any time. Actual ongoing participants will be capped at a fixed level prior to opening and be based on "future participants" on a first come first served basis.
